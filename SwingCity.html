Swing City

A 2.5D 1v1 Claw Game Made for 48 Hour Global Game Jam 2020 (3rd Place on Site).

Swing City is a game that was made by myself and my team at the Global Game Jam 2020 in The Foundry over the course of 48 hours. The theme for the jam was "Repair". It's a fun physics-based 1v1 claw game where you compete with a friend to rebuild the highest pile of blocks while dealing with bombs and a menacing opponent. 

My role during this game jame was to create the main arm mechanics. This included implementing rope style physics for the swinging and creating a claw which could pick up the objects in the game. We had a lot of fun creating this game and all scripts, art and sound was made on site during the jam.
